The Science of 3I/ATLAS

rocket_launch

Interstellar Origin

3I/ATLAS is only the third confirmed interstellar object detected in our solar system. Its hyperbolic trajectory confirms it originated from beyond our sun's gravitational influence, offering a rare sample from another star system.

science

Unique Composition

Early spectral analysis suggests a composition rich in carbon-chain molecules and a surprising lack of water ice compared to our native comets. This hints at a formation environment very different from our own.
